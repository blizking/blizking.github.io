New Era Industries was incorporated in the year 1956 with a vision to be a world leader in its field. From a humble beginning manufacturing machine tools, the company has now emerged as a world class player in the field of Biscuit Manufacturing Machinery. The company's sprawling infrastructure is located 300 kms north of New Delhi, the capital city of India.

Innovative technologies in mechanical & electrical engineering has been the strength of the company. Determination, flexibility and commitments form the pillars of success. Meeting customized requirements is of paramount importance.

How it's made

Biscuit making is a fine art, Minute changes in proportions of the ingredients, baking time, baking speed, etc. can affect the taste, form and texture of the end product. Our lines and food technologists specialize in optimizing these moving factors to ensure that you achieve consistent results all the time. Learn more about the process here.

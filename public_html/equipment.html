FeedingDough Feeding Systems

Dough Feeding Systems

  • Process
    • Mixed Dough is unloaded from the Mixer to a Dough Trough
    • Mixed Dough can also be dumped directly into the Dough Feeding System
    • Mixed dough passes through the Feeder to process and make it into Brick Shape suitable for Hard Dough and Crackers or it is formed in small Kibbles suitable for Rotary Moulding Machine
    • Various designs of Feeders are available in order to maintain the standing time depending on the process if required
    • This dough is then passed through a metal detection conveyor
    • Contaminated dough is then rejected off the line and rest if fed into the process
  • Application
    • Soft Dough
    • Hard Dough & Crackers
  • Salient Features
    • Length of the conveyor can be customized according to capacity and line requirement
    • Dough level sensors at the forming station gives feedback to the system in order to have correct levels of feeding in the hoppers
  • Options
    • CE/UL Certification
  • Controls
    • PLC System with Centralized HMI display
    • Recipe Management System
FeedingDough Feeding Systems

Dough Feeding Systems with Star Roll Sheeter

  • Process
    • Dough can directly be dropped into the hopper of Star Roll Sheeter or a Tilter or Lifter Cum Tilter can be used depending on the Layout
    • Star Roll Sheeter is deployed to produce dough lumps that can be directly fed to cutting line or passed through a rotary kibbler to break down into small pieces for Moulding lines.
    • System can be foreseen with a Metal detector and Rejection System.

FormingScrap Lift Conveyor

Scrap Lift Conveyor

  • Technology
    • This unit is to take off scrap from the sheeting coming from Rotary Cutting Unit.
    • The scrap lifted from this unit is delivered to a narrow inclined conveyor on the non operator side which takes it back to dough feed hopper.
    • The return point can be foreseen with a reciprocating distributor to uniformly distribute the scrap being returned across the width of the processing system.
  • Application
    • Separates scrap from product and transfers it to a cross conveyor which further delivers to scrap return system for re processing
  • Options
    • Pneumatic Actuator to lifting and running positions.
    • Light barrier for operator's safety in case of pneumatically actuated lifting conveyor.
  • Controls
    • PLC System with Centralized HMI display
    • Recipe Management System

BakingDirect Forced Convection Oven

Direct Forced Convection Oven

  • Technology
    • Direct Heated Forced Convection Oven.
  • Product Application
    • Soft Biscuits
    • Cookies
    • Wire Cut Products
    • Hard Biscuits
    • Crackers
  • Fuel Options
    • Gas Fired Burners
  • Band Options
    • Solid Steel Band
    • Z47 Wiremesh
    • CB5 Balanced Weave Heavy wire Mesh
    • F4015/K-ST Wiremesh
  • Salient Features
    • High Efficiency due to direct convection technology
    • Flue Gas coming from Torch Burner is mixed directly with recirculation air of bake chamber and then directed on to ducts placed above and below the band. The ducts are thus pressurised and the hot air escapes thorough the nozzle holes which are placed uniformly all across the length and width of both the ducts. The air escaping from these nozzles at a controllable velocity directly impinges on to the product travelling on the band.
    • Humidity control in each heating zone by a steam extraction fan
    • Modular Construction - Very easy to install & commission on site
    • Floating baking chamber design prevents thermal stress on oven frame & Chamber
  • Accesories
    • Band Drive Unit & Band Tensioning Systems - different designs to match band selection.
    • Band Tracking Systems - different designs to match band selection.
    • Delivery End Extraction Hood
    • Choice between various burner manufacturers
  • Controls
    • PLC System with PID Controls & HMI display
    • Recipe Management System

BakingIndirect Radiant Cyclotherm Oven

Indirect Radiant Cyclotherm Oven

  • Technology
    • In-Direct Heated. Heat transfer through radiation of energy from radiator tubes above and below the band.
  • Product Application
    • Soft Biscuits
    • Cookies
    • Wire Cut Products
  • Fuel Options
    • Gas Fired Burners
    • Oil Fired Burners
    • Gas Heavy Oil Burners
  • Band Options
    • Solid Steel Band
    • Z47 Wiremesh
    • CB5 Balanced Weave Heavy wire Mesh
    • F4015/K-ST Wiremesh
  • Salient Features
    • Floating baking chamber design prevents thermal stress on oven frame & Chamber
    • Heated flue gas from burner is recalculated in radiator tubes located above and below the band. These tubes get heated up and radiate energy to product travelling in between.
    • Heat distribution & balancing across the width easily adjustable by dampers in each tube.
    • Humidity control in each heating zone by steam extraction damper
  • Accessories
    • Band Drive Unit & Band Tensioning Systems - different designs to match band selection.
    • Band Tracking Systems - different designs to match band selection.
    • Delivery End Extraction Hood
  • Controls
    • PLC System with PID Controls & HMI display
    • Recipe Management System

FormingScrap Lift Conveyor

Scrap Lift Conveyor

  • Technology
    • This unit is to take off scrap from the sheeting coming from Rotary Cutting Unit.
    • The scrap lifted from this unit is delivered to a narrow inclined conveyor on the non operator side which takes it back to dough feed hopper.
    • The return point can be foreseen with a reciprocating distributor to uniformly distribute the scrap being returned across the width of the processing system.
  • Application
    • Separates scrap from product and transfers it to a cross conveyor which further delivers to scrap return system for re processing
  • Options
    • Pneumatic Actuator to lifting and running positions.
    • Light barrier for operator's safety in case of pneumatically actuated lifting conveyor.

BakingDirect Forced Convection Oven

Direct Forced Convection Oven

  • Technology
    • Direct Heated Forced Convection Oven.
  • Product Application
    • Soft Biscuits
    • Cookies
    • Wire Cut Products
    • Hard Biscuits
    • Crackers
  • Fuel Options
    • Gas Fired Burners
  • Band Options
    • Solid Steel Band
    • Z47 Wiremesh
    • CB5 Balanced Weave Heavy wire Mesh
    • F4015/K-ST Wiremesh
  • Salient Features
    • High Efficiency due to direct convection technology
    • Flue Gas coming from Torch Burner is mixed directly with recirculation air of bake chamber and then directed on to ducts placed above and below the band. The ducts are thus pressurised and the hot air escapes thorough the nozzle holes which are placed uniformly all across the length and width of both the ducts. The air escaping from these nozzles at a controllable velocity directly impinges on to the product travelling on the band.
    • Humidity control in each heating zone by a steam extraction fan
    • Modular Construction - Very easy to install & commission on site
    • Floating baking chamber design prevents thermal stress on oven frame & Chamber
  • Accesories
    • Band Drive Unit & Band Tensioning Systems - different designs to match band selection.
    • Band Tracking Systems - different designs to match band selection.
    • Delivery End Extraction Hood
    • Choice between various burner manufacturers
